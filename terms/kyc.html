KYC and Verification

SilentFlare may require identity verification before or during use of the service, especially for restricted-region users, high-risk activity, payment risk, abuse reports, or legal compliance.

KYC Level 1

KYC Level 1 may require a passport, national identity card, driving licence, or residence permit, plus facial verification.

KYC Level 2

KYC Level 2 may require proof of address, such as a bank statement, utility bill, government letter, residence certificate, or other document accepted by SilentFlare.

KYC Level 3

KYC Level 3 may require enhanced due diligence, source-of-funds explanation, business information, additional identity evidence, or manual compliance review.

False information

False, forged, inconsistent, or misleading verification information may result in immediate denial, suspension, termination, data retention for abuse review, and refusal of future service.
