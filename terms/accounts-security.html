Accounts and Security

The user is responsible for all activity under their account, credentials, API keys, tokens, hosted content, traffic, and automation.

Required conduct

  • Keep passwords, API keys, and recovery methods secure.
  • Use accurate account, billing, and contact information.
  • Notify SilentFlare promptly if an account, token, key, or system may be compromised.
  • Do not share, resell, transfer, or sublicense accounts unless SilentFlare gives written permission.

Compromised accounts

SilentFlare may reset credentials, revoke tokens, suspend resources, require verification, or preserve logs when compromise or suspicious activity is detected.
