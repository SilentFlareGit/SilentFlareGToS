Contact and Notices

For legal, abuse, compliance, billing, privacy, or security requests, contact the SilentFlare operator through the official project contact channel.

Notice requirements

Requests should include enough information to identify the account, service, domain, IP address, invoice, abuse event, or legal basis involved.

Abuse reports

Abuse reports should include timestamps, logs, headers, URLs, screenshots, affected systems, and any relevant evidence.

Response

SilentFlare may prioritize requests based on legal urgency, infrastructure risk, account impact, and completeness of the submitted information.
